Detailed Description

Convenience class for holding pixel contrast weights.

These are used in various CRF image segmentation models (see drwnSegImageInstance) with pairwise neighbourhood penalties taking the form where is the mean square-difference between neighbouring pixel colours and is the distance between pixels p and q.
